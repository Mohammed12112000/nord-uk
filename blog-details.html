What we offer

Core Services

Delivering engineering excellence across sectors with end-to-end structural and construction support.

Structural Design

Complete structural design for RCC and steel structures across industrial, commercial, residential, and institutional projects — compliant with AISC, BS & Indian Standards.

Steel Structure Design

Design of industrial steel buildings, warehouses, mezzanines, pipe racks, trusses, and heavy structures — weight-optimised and fabrication-feasible per AISC, BS & Indian Standards.

Steel Detailing, PEB & Industrial Shed

Comprehensive steel detailing for PMB buildings, industrial sheds, and complex structures. GA drawings, shop drawings, and erection drawings per AISC, BS & Indian Standards.

Connection Design & Detailing

Bolted and welded steel connections — base plates, anchor bolts, and moment connections. Engineered to AISC, BS & Indian Standards for safety and fabrication efficiency.

Precast & RCC Rebar Detailing

Reinforcement detailing and bar bending schedules for precast and RCC elements — footings, slabs, beams, and columns per AISC, BS & Indian Standards.

Foundation & Equipment Base Design

Specialised foundation design for buildings, machinery bases, and heavy steel structures — soil and load-compliant, economical solutions per AISC, BS & Indian Standards.

Retrofitting & Structural Strengthening

Assessment and strengthening of existing RCC and steel structures — industrial retrofitting and load upgrades engineered per AISC, BS & Indian Standards for safe, reliable performance.

Project & Construction Support

Technical support during fabrication and construction — drawing reviews, coordination, and site guidance ensuring execution aligns with AISC, BS & Indian Standards.

How we work

Technical Capabilities

The tools and methodologies that power our delivery.

Advanced Structural Analysis

Structural analysis using STAAD Pro, Tekla Structural Designer, and RCDC for steel and RCC — safe, optimised, and code-compliant analytical models.

Steel Detailing Software Expertise

Tekla Structures and advanced detailing tools for fabrication-ready shop drawings, GA drawings, and erection drawings with precision and seamless steel execution.

BIM & Digital Coordination

BIM-based coordination with Revit and Navisworks for clash detection, model review, and multi-discipline integration — streamlined workflows and reduced site conflicts.

FEM & Advanced Simulation

Finite Element Modelling using ANSYS and Abaqus for complex structural behaviour, stress analysis, and heavy structural validation — driving performance-led engineering.

Connection & Design Validation

Advanced connection design validation and structural checks using specialist software and manual review — ensuring compliance with AISC, BS & Indian Standards.

Proof Checking & Peer Review

Independent proof checking and design validation for structural safety, stability, and code compliance — ensuring accuracy and reliability across steel and RCC projects.

Digital Collaboration & Workflow

Cloud-based collaboration and structured workflows for real-time coordination with clients, architects, and contractors — ensuring transparency and efficient project delivery.

Integrated Engineering & Data Management

Structured project management, drawing control, and integrated engineering workflow ensuring organised documentation, faster delivery, and consistent quality across projects.
