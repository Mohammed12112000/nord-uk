What we do

What we offer Core Services

Delivering engineering excellence across sectors with end-to-end support across the project lifecycle from concept design through to construction coordination and delivery support.

01. Structural Designing

Comprehensive structural design for steel and reinforced concrete systems across industrial, commercial, residential, and institutional projects. Designs are developed with safety, efficiency, and buildability in accordance with Eurocodes, American codes, and British Standards.

02. Steel Structure Design

Design of industrial steel structures including warehouses, production facilities, mezzanine floors, pipe racks, trusses, and heavy steel systems. Solutions are optimised for structural efficiency, fabrication feasibility, and compliance with international standards.

03. Steel Detailing & Fabrication Engineering

Production of coordinated GA drawings, shop drawings, and erection drawings for steel structures and prefabricated buildings. Deliverables are developed for accuracy, constructability, and seamless integration with fabrication and site execution workflows.

04. Precast & RCC Rebar Detailing

Detailed reinforcement drawings and bar bending schedules for reinforced concrete structures including foundations, slabs, beams, columns, and precast elements, ensuring precision, coordination, and site-ready execution.

05. Structural Assessment & Retrofitting

Evaluation, strengthening, and upgrading of existing steel and reinforced concrete structures. We deliver practical retrofit solutions that improve safety, extend service life, and ensure compliance with current design standards.

06. BIM & Coordination Services

BIM-based modelling and coordination to enhance design integration, minimise clashes, and improve collaboration between consultants, contractors, and fabricators across all project stages.

07. Quantity Surveying & Cost Management

Accurate quantity take-offs, cost estimation, and value engineering services to support budgeting, procurement planning, and effective cost control throughout the project lifecycle.

08. Project & Construction Management Support

Project planning and coordination support including Gantt chart scheduling, progress tracking, and milestone management to ensure efficient execution. We also support design interfaces, stakeholder coordination, and construction-phase activities to reduce delays and maintain programme alignment through site completion.

How we work

Technical Capabilities

Advanced tools, methodologies, and engineering systems driving accurate and coordinated project delivery.

01. Advanced Structural Engineering

Analysis & Design Excellence: Structural analysis and design using STAAD Pro, Tekla Structural Designer, and RCDC for steel and reinforced concrete systems. Solutions are delivered in compliance with Eurocodes, American codes (AISC, ACI), and British Standards, supported by advanced simulation for complex behaviour.

02. Steel Detailing & Fabrication Engineering

Precision for Construction: High-accuracy steel detailing using Tekla Structures to produce fabrication-ready shop, GA, and erection drawings. Outputs are developed in accordance with AISC, Eurocodes, and British Standards for constructability and seamless design-to-site execution.

03. BIM & Digital Coordination

Integrated Project Modelling: BIM-based coordination using Revit and Navisworks for clash detection, model integration, and multi-discipline collaboration. We ensure alignment across stakeholders to improve clarity and reduce conflicts through all stages of delivery.

04. Engineering Validation & Project Control

Assurance & Delivery Accuracy: Advanced FEM-based analysis using ANSYS and Abaqus, alongside connection design validation, independent proof checking, and structured engineering workflows. Deliverables are verified against Eurocodes, American standards, and British Standards for reliable execution.
