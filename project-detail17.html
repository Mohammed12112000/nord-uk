Project Description:

Nord Engineers provided Structural Design & Detailing for the ENRIC fiber godown, an industrial steel structure. Plan area is 44 m × 35 m (~1,540 m²) with multi-span steel framing, internal columns, and a curved/pitched roof system. Steel tonnage is 217 MT.

  • ScopeStructural Design & Detailing
  • Plan Area44 m × 35 m (~1,540 m²)
  • SystemMulti-span steel framing, internal columns, curved/pitched roof
  • Steel Tonnage217 MT

Engineering Complexity

  • Integrated industrial godown with internal service zones
  • Curved roof geometry requiring deflection control
  • Dense column grid with optimized load transfer to foundations
  • Coordinated superstructure–foundation design for industrial loads

Challenge

Integrating internal service zones with a curved roof and dense column grid required careful deflection control and optimized load path to foundations.

Solution

Nord Engineers delivered coordinated superstructure and foundation design with optimized load transfer, curved roof deflection control, and 217 MT of structural steel for the 1,540 m² godown.
