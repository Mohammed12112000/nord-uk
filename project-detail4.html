Overview:

Nord Engineers is proud to be the structural engineering firm behind the "Code-Blue Hospital" project, a groundbreaking development in modern healthcare. Located in Medchal, Karnataka, this project exemplifies our commitment to innovative structural solutions and excellence.

  • Floors B+G+5
  • Area 48,000 Sq. Ft
  • Project Cost₹6.5 cr.
  • Year 2023 - (Ongoing)

Project Highlights

  • Innovative Design: The hospital features wide column-free spans and irregular-shaped cut-outs within the slabs, creating an open and flexible environment that supports advanced healthcare delivery.
  • Distinctive Facade: A unique façade enhances the building’s aesthetic appeal and stands as a symbol of modern healthcare architecture.
  • Comprehensive Solutions: Our bespoke structural solutions ensure safety, stability, and innovation across the expansive 48,000-square-foot facility.
  • Collaborative Approach: Through seamless collaboration with the client, architects, and project management consultancy, we have overcome challenges and delivered a project that reflects our dedication to excellence
  • The "Code-Blue Hospital" is a testament to Nord Engineers' commitment to pushing the boundaries of structural engineering, creating spaces that inspire and uplift communities

Challenge

Creating Wide Column-Free Spans The design included expansive column-free spans to create open and flexible spaces, critical for advanced healthcare delivery. Maintaining structural stability over such spans posed significant engineering challenges.

Solution

Nord Engineers employed advanced Post-Tension (PT) slab systems, enabling the construction of wide spans without intermediate columns. This ensured both structural integrity and the functional flexibility required for healthcare operations.
