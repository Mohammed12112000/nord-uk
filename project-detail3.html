Overview:

Nord Engineers is proud to present the New-Life Hospital project, a landmark in Latur, Maharashtra. As the tallest building in the region, this project represents a significant advancement in local infrastructure. Our role as the structural consultancy involved pioneering engineering solutions to create a state-of-the-art healthcare facility.

  • Floors G+8
  • Area 60,000 Sq. Ft
  • Project Cost ₹16 cr
  • Features 15-Meter column free spans, PT Slabs, Tallest Building of

    Latur

  • Year 2023-24

Project Highlights

The New-Life Hospital project stands as a testament to Nord Engineers' unwavering commitment to safety, stability, and innovation in large-scale construction. Featuring unique architectural elements such as 15-meter column-free spans and PT slabs, the design ensures expansive and flexible interiors ideal for healthcare environments. The structure's distinctive façade and irregular slab cut-outs enhance both functionality and aesthetic appeal. Through close collaboration with the client, architects, and project management teams, this project exemplifies our dedication to engineering excellence and innovative solutions.

Challenge

Designing 15-Meter Column-Free Spans Creating 15-meter column-free spans in a healthcare setting posed challenges in ensuring structural stability while maintaining open, flexible interiors essential for hospital functionality.

Solution

Nord Engineers incorporated advanced Post-Tension (PT) slab technology to support the expansive spans without compromising on safety or stability. Detailed structural analysis and reinforcement designs were employed to distribute loads effectively and minimize deflection over time.
