What We Deliver

01

Structural Design

Design solutions for steel, reinforced concrete, and pre-engineered buildings developed in compliance with international codes and project-specific requirements, ensuring safety, efficiency, and buildability.

02

Steel Detailing & BIM

Accurate fabrication-ready steel and rebar detailing supported by BIM-based coordination to ensure clarity, clash-free models, and seamless integration between design, fabrication, and construction.

03

Quantity Take-offs & Cost Services

Detailed quantity take-offs, cost estimation, and value engineering support to assist in budgeting, procurement planning, and effective project cost control.

04

Design Review & Project Coordination

Independent design review, proof checking, and multidisciplinary coordination services to ensure compliance, technical accuracy, and alignment across all project stakeholders.

05

Project Management Support

Technical project coordination and management support to improve communication between consultants, contractors, and fabricators, ensuring efficient workflow, reduced delays, and smooth project execution.
